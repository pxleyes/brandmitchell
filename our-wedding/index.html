The Happy Couple

How we got here

A mutual friend set Brandon and Christy up for their first date during their senior year at high school. Christy attended Winter Park High school and ran cross-country while Brandon was a member of the yearbook and debate team at Trinity Preparatory school. Their first date was at Starbucks and both non-coffee drinking kids ordered peppermint hot cocoas (still one of their favorites) on Tuskawilla Road.

Their parents lived a few neighborhoods down Aloma Avenue from each other. Soon, they were cruising around Winter Park listening to Sister Hazel and going on date nights as often as possible. On December 3, 2002 the couple decided to “go steady”. After prom at each other’s high school, Brandon, who is from a long line of Gator graduates, found out that he got into the University of Florida while Christy got into the University of Central Florida. After 2 years of seeing each other over summers and at Gator football games, Christy transferred to the University of Florida to finish her degree.

After living together in college, they moved to Jacksonville to continue Christy’s education. Unfortunately, in 2009, Brandon and Christy moved back to Orlando when Brandon’s dad was diagnosed with brain cancer. That was an extremely difficult year but it brought the couple closer together in the end. Brandon is currently working at 352 Media Company, a web design, company based and Gainesville, while Christy is pursuing her second bachelors in nursing. After 9 years of dating, they will officially be married on October 13, 2012.
